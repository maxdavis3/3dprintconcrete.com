How We Build

01

Robotic Extrusion

A gantry or robotic arm follows a digital path, extruding concrete in precise layers to build walls, floors, and curved forms directly from an architectural CAD model.

02

Residential Homes

From 400 sq ft emergency units to full 2,000 sq ft family homes, our systems print complete structural shells in under 48 hours at a fraction of conventional costs.

Commercial & Infrastructure

Offices, warehouses, bridge supports, retaining walls, and public infrastructure built with unmatched geometric freedom and structural performance.

MIX DESIGN

Engineered Concrete Mixes

Not all concrete is print-ready. Our proprietary mix designs are engineered for pumpability, open time, and rapid layer-on-layer buildability without formwork. We offer a range of mixes for different structural and sustainability requirements.

  • ● Portland Cement Printable Mix (35–50 MPa)
  • ● Geopolymer Concrete (Low-carbon, 40+ MPa)
  • ● Fiber-Reinforced Printable Mortar
  • ● Lightweight Foam Concrete (<900 kg/m³)
  • ● Recycled Aggregate Blends
  • ● High-Performance Ultra-Fine Concrete
