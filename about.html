About 3D Printconcrete

Pioneering robotic construction technology to make housing faster, greener, and more affordable

OUR STORY

Who We Are

3D Printconcrete is a Chicago-based construction technology company specializing in robotic concrete extrusion. We operate large-format gantry and robotic arm printing systems capable of producing entire building structures — walls, floors, stairs — directly from architectural models, without traditional formwork.

The global housing shortage demands a new approach to construction. Our technology cuts build times by up to 70%, reduces material waste dramatically, and makes custom architectural geometries economically feasible for the first time. We work with developers, municipalities, disaster relief organizations, and homeowners who are ready to build differently.

Our Mission

To accelerate the transition to robotic construction by making 3D concrete printing reliable, scalable, and accessible. We believe that better technology is the path to solving the housing crisis — building more with less labor, less waste, and less time, without compromising structural integrity or design ambition.

Our Vision

A world where a family can move into a high-quality, energy-efficient printed home within days of breaking ground. We envision construction sites where robots and humans work in concert, and where complex architectural forms are no longer cost-prohibitive for everyday builders.
